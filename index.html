Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

🛡️

Mise is designed with your privacy in mind. Most of your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

Mise is a personal pantry management application. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the Mise development team. "You" refers to the person using the app.

2. What Data We Collect

Mise collects the following information, all of which is stored locally on your device:

  • Profile information — your name and, optionally, your email address. These are entered during onboarding and used only to personalise your in-app experience. Your email is never used for marketing.
  • Pantry data — ingredient names, quantities, storage locations, and expiry dates that you add manually.
  • Shopping list — items and quantities you add to your shopping list.
  • Dietary preferences — any dietary restrictions or preferences you select (e.g. vegetarian, gluten-free).
  • Taste profile — ingredient ratings you provide to help personalise recipe suggestions.
  • App settings — your notification and shopping list preferences.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the features of the app:

  • Displaying and managing your pantry and shopping list
  • Sending local push notifications about expiring items (if enabled)
  • Personalising recipe suggestions based on your dietary preferences and taste profile
  • Showing your name and profile within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data collected by Mise is stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

You can delete all stored data at any time by going to Settings → Danger Zone → Reset App. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

5. AI Features

Mise is building AI-powered features including recipe recommendations and pantry analysis. When these features are enabled, ingredient and preference data may be sent to a third-party AI provider (such as Anthropic) solely to generate a response. This data is used only to fulfil your request and is not retained or used to train AI models.

AI features will be clearly labelled within the app and will require explicit interaction to activate. You will not be opted in automatically.

6. Push Notifications

Mise uses local push notifications to remind you about expiring pantry items. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings or through your device's notification settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Mise does not currently integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes, this policy will be updated and you will be notified within the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Mise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address this.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored by Mise (visible within the app at all times)
  • Correct any data by editing your profile or pantry entries
  • Delete all data using the Reset App function in Settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you within the app. Continued use of Mise after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app's listing on the App Store or Google Play.
